Article 110

Examination of appeals Amended by the Act revising the European Patent Convention of 29.11.2000. See decisions/opinions of the Enlarged Board of Appeal G 9/91, G 10/91, G 10/93, G 3/99 (Annex I).

R. 100 102 111 113

If the appeal is admissible, the Board of Appeal shall examine whether the appeal is allowable. The examination of the appeal shall be conducted in accordance with the Implementing Regulations.
