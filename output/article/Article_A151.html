Article 151

The European Patent Office as a receiving Office See notice from the EPO concerning the requirements to be observed when filing an international application with the EPO as a PCT receiving Office, OJ EPO 2014, A33 ; and the decision of the President of the EPO concerning the filing of international applications with the EPO acting as receiving Office using ePCT-Filing, OJ EPO 2014, A107 .

Art. 35 153
R. 157

The European Patent Office shall act as a receiving Office within the meaning of the PCT, in accordance with the Implementing Regulations. Article 75, paragraph 2, shall apply.
