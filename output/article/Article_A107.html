Article 107

Persons entitled to appeal and to be parties to appeal proceedings See decisions of the Enlarged Board of Appeal G 1/88, G 2/91, G 4/91, G 9/92, G 1/99, G 3/99, G 3/03, G 2/04, G 3/04, G 1/12 (Annex I).

R. 101 111

Any party to proceedings adversely affected by a decision may appeal. Any other parties to the proceedings shall be parties to the appeal proceedings as of right.
