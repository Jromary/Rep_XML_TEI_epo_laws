Article 15

Departments entrusted with the procedure See opinion of the Enlarged Board of Appeal G 1/02 (Annex I).

Art. 16 22 143
R. 8 13

To carry out the procedures laid down in this Convention, the following shall be set up within the European Patent Office:

(a)

a Receiving Section;

(b)

Search Divisions;

(c)

Examining Divisions;

(d)

Opposition Divisions;

(e)

a Legal Division;

(f)

Boards of Appeal;

(g)

an Enlarged Board of Appeal.
