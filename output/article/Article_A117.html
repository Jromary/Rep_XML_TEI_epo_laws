Article 117

Means and taking of evidence Amended by the Act revising the European Patent Convention of 29.11.2000. See decisions/opinions of the Enlarged Board of Appeal G 3/89, G 11/91, G 4/95 (Annex I).

R. 4 117 124 150

(1)

In proceedings before the European Patent Office the means of giving or obtaining evidence shall include the following:

(a)

hearing the parties;

(b)

requests for information;

(c)

production of documents;

(d)

hearing witnesses;

(e)

opinions by experts;

(f)

inspection;

(g)

sworn statements in writing.

(2)

The procedure for taking such evidence shall be laid down in the Implementing Regulations.
