Rule 162

Claims incurring fees Amended by decision of the Administrative Council CA/D 12/10 of 26.10.2010 (OJ EPO 2010, 634), entered into force on 01.05.2011.

Art. 153
R. 45

(1)

If the application documents on which the European grant procedure is to be based comprise more than fifteen claims, claims fees shall be paid for the sixteenth and each subsequent claim as laid down in the Rules relating to Fees within the period under Rule 159, paragraph 1.

(2) Amended by decision of the Administrative Council CA/D 17/16 of 14.12.2016 ( OJ EPO 2016, A102 ), entered into force on 01.01.2017

If the claims fees are not paid in due time, they may still be paid within the period under Rule 161, paragraph 1 or Rule 161, paragraph 2 , as the case may be . If within this period amended claims are filed, the claims fees due shall be computed on the basis of such amended claims and shall be paid within this period .

(3)

Any claims fees paid within the period under paragraph 1 and in excess of those due under paragraph 2, second sentence, shall be refunded.

(4)

Where a claims fee is not paid in due time, the claim concerned shall be deemed to be abandoned.
